    • Shooty Squad

    Shooty squad is an online multiplayer first person shooter I developed and released to steam. This project was created and coded solely by myself, over the course of 6 months. Most of the art assets are also produced by me, with game design and level design also my responsibility. This is the first title I shipped as a solo indie developer !

    Smash Crusaders

    A BreakOut-meets-Endless Runner adventure game in development at MassDiGI released to Play Store and App Store. I was the lead programmer of this project and managed two other programmers. I was responsible for Ad implementation, all the core systems of the game and building for iOS and Android

    BlockTale

    This is a game built for both Android and iOS, this was a game that I created with a one month deadline. Available on App Store and Play Store. The aim of this game is to build a tower as tall as you possibly can without losing your lives. Players can strategically glue blocks together to make the structure more stable.
    Key Features Include: Online Leaderboards, Facebook friend Leaderboards, Rewarded video ads.
